Amazon Smile

AmazonSmile is a website operated by Amazon with
the same products, prices, and shopping features as
Amazon.com. The difference is that when you shop
on AmazonSmile, the AmazonSmile Foundation
will donate 0.5% of the purchase price of eligible products to PAAA!
